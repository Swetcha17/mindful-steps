Mindful Steps

Our Team

At Mindful Steps, we believe that everyone deserves access to the mental, emotional, and physical benefits of nature — whether on a forest trail or in a VR headset. Our project bridges the gap between the primordial power of forest therapy with modern mindfulness practices, cutting-edge technology, and wellness mindsets of the 21st century.

Developed by and for the students, staff, and faculty at Lehigh University, we have a strong desire to bring calm and connection to the members of the Lehigh community. Our work offers a variety of mediums through which one can experience the benefits of forest therapy:

  • Short on time and needing a quick reset? Create your own looping forest walking route, optimized for walkability and physiological benefit.
  • Wanting a more thorough experience in the forest? Sign up for one of our guided forest walking sessions.
  • Looking for all the same benefits without walking? Try out our virtual reality forest therapy experience.
